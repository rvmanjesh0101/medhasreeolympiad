About Us

A place well designed to give excellent education and to create healthy minds.

OUR MISSION

Is to sow the seeds of knowledge, curiosity, empathy and courage in the minds of our young generation and transform them into a successful individuals.

OUR VISION

Is to grow into an educational institution where we focus on cognitive and emotional development of a child who can bring a revolution in the development of India by keeping their roots firm on the rich Indian heritage abundant in its values and tradition.

  • Enrichment Activities

    Children are exposed to various means of knowledge in different ways to explore. We conduct monthly activities like color spatter day, regional & national festivals, nutria deal, weekly activities and observe International days. Etc.,

  • Audio Visual Play

    To enlighten the harmonious development of pupils it is necessary to use all the senses. So here in our school we provide Audio visual play way method of teaching were in both physical and mental aspects of the child is enhanced through audio visual method of teaching.,

  • Computer Labs

    oday’s world is evoking in digitalization, to inculcate the awareness of digitalization we do have well furnished updated computer lab facilities.

  • Sports and Games

    Along with the mental and reasoning development of the child. A child also needs physical development too. So we have specious play ground with whole lot of sports material for conducting sports and games.
